1. Introduction

Thasri Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how data is collected, used, and protected when using the app.

2. Data You Provide

We collect account details you submit such as name, email, and profile image for sign-in and sync features.

3. Location Data

Location is used to calculate prayer times, Qibla direction, and nearby mosque features. Location may be processed locally and transmitted securely to required services for religious time calculations.

4. Third-Party Services

We use services including Supabase (authentication/data), Aladhan API (prayer data), and AdMob (ads in free experience). Only required data is shared to provide these features.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Data is retained while your account is active. You may request deletion from in-app settings or by contacting support. Account data is removed from our systems after deletion processing.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us.
